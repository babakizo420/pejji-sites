Our Story

Born from a Love of Nigerian Food

Amala House started in 2018 with a simple belief: Nigerian cuisine deserves a stage as grand as its flavours. What began as a small kitchen in Surulere has grown into one of Victoria Island's most celebrated dining destinations.

Our chefs draw from recipes passed down through generations, using locally sourced ingredients and traditional cooking methods. Every bowl of amala is hand-turned. Every pot of egusi is slow-simmered. Every plate that leaves our kitchen carries the soul of Nigerian cooking.

We are not just a restaurant. We are a gathering place where families celebrate, friends reconnect, and every guest leaves feeling at home.
